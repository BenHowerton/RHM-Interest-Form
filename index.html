Program Overview

The Resort and Hospitality Management (RHM) program is designed to prepare students for leadership roles in hotels, resorts, theme parks, convention centers, and event planning companies. Combining classroom instruction with hands-on experience, the RHM program empowers you with the skills to succeed in today’s competitive global tourism industry.

With an emphasis on business fundamentals, service excellence, and hospitality operations, students graduate with real-world readiness and access to powerful industry networks.

Real Industry Experience

Business Core + Hospitality

In-Demand Career Path

Graduate ready to lead in hotels, resorts, events, tourism boards, and destination marketing.
